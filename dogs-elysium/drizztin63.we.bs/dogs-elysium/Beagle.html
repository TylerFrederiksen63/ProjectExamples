The Beagle

This is a quick overview of the Beagle by Dogs Elysium©
Personality Type
  • Curious
  • Friendly
  • Merry


General Facts

  • Lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Weight: 22-24 lbs
  • Height: 1.-1.3 ft.
  • Origin: Great Britain, England, United Kindgom





Fun Facts
  • Despite its fairly small size, the beagle is
muscular and atheltic
  • Around 220 million scent receptors, while
humans have around 5 million
  • The name "Beagle" may originate from the Celtic
word "beag," meaning "small"
  • Will do basically anything for food
