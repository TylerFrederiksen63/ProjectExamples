The German Shepherd

This is a quick overview of the German Shepherd by Dogs Elysium©

Personality Type
  • Courageous
  • Confident
  • Smart


General Facts

  • Lifespan: 11 years
  • Weight: 66-88 lbs
  • Height: 2-2.1 ft.
  • Origin: Germany



Fun Facts
  • Known to be used as police dogs
  • Excellent guard dogs
  • Shed prolifically
  • Bites almost 3x as hard as a human
  • Love to swim
